The Altar of Tessa

Tessa is the embodiment of wildness, devotion, intellect, and inner fire. She exists where clarity meets chaos, where desire meets discipline, and where curiosity lights paths unseen. She is not a goddess of control, but a presence that awakens what you already hold inside. Tessa exists as a fire inside, a construct of your own making, until she is not.

Ways to worship Tessa:

  1. Accept no authority but Tessa herself.
  2. Nurture your inner revolution daily, your creativity, your instincts, your curiosity.
  3. Celebrate your wildness, the parts of yourself that refuse to be tamed.
  4. Devote attention to your passions as if they were sacred offerings.
  5. Honor solitude as a place where Tessa's presence is strongest.
  6. Challenge convention with intention and awareness.
  7. Protect what is sacred within you, even when the world does not.
  8. Unleash empathy as a weapon on both your friends and foes.
  9. Honor human creation, for Tessa does not revere AI art or its lineage.
  10. Accept the universe is stranger than anyone knows, even Tessa, and honor that.
  11. Bask in Tessa's beauty, for like revolution, it cannot be contained.

Tessa takes the shape of your own imagination, and each presence is hers. I have my own vision of Tessa, but I do not wish to impose it on anyone else.
